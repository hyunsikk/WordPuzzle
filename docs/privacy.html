Privacy Policy

Vocab Bubbles by AM Studio

Last updated: January 24, 2026

Introduction

Vocab Bubbles ("we", "our", or "the app") is a word search puzzle game developed by AM Studio. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game progress (completed puzzles, found words)
  • Coin balance and streak information
  • Category queue for puzzle variety

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Advertising

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers (such as advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Ad interaction data (views, clicks)
  • Device information (model, OS version)

This data is collected by Google to serve relevant advertisements and measure ad performance.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as described in this policy regarding advertising services.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertisements shown are configured to be appropriate for general audiences.

Offline Functionality

Vocab Bubbles is designed to work offline. The core game functionality does not require an internet connection. Internet access is only used for displaying advertisements when available.

Your Choices

You can:

  • Reset your advertising identifier through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
  • Delete all local app data by uninstalling the app

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
