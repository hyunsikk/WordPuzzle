Privacy Policy

Word Puzzle: SAT Vocab

The short version: Word Puzzle stores all your progress locally on your device. We don't collect your data. Third-party ads (AdMob) may collect advertising identifiers.

Information We Collect

Word Puzzle itself collects no personal information. We have no servers, no analytics, and no user accounts. Your vocabulary progress, game stats, and settings are stored locally on your device only.

Data Storage

All app data (unlocked levels, scores, preferences) is stored locally using on-device storage. This data never leaves your phone. We cannot access it, and neither can anyone else.

Third-Party Services

Word Puzzle uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA on iOS)
  • Approximate location (based on IP address)
  • Ad interaction data

AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings → Privacy → Tracking on iOS).

No Account Required

Word Puzzle does not require account creation, email, or any personal information to play.

Data Deletion

Deleting the app removes all your progress and data permanently. You can also reset progress from within the app settings.

Children's Privacy

Word Puzzle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. AdMob serves age-appropriate ads when child-directed treatment is enabled.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the changes here. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at support@teamam.dev or hyunsikkim@gmail.com.
